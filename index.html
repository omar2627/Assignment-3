    Important Tips Before Buying a Dog

    Make sure you follow these steps to avoid scams and get a healthy puppy.

    Buy from a Trusted Breeder

    Avoid random online sellers. Always choose a registered and well-reviewed breeder.

    Check Health Conditions

    Inspect the puppy's eyes, ears, nose, and fur. Avoid sick or weak-looking dogs.

    Ask for Vaccination Records

    A responsible breeder should provide medical records. No records? It’s a red flag!

    Beware of Low Prices

    If the price seems too good to be true, it's probably a scam or a sick puppy.

    Choose the Right Breed

    Not all breeds suit every home. Research which breed fits your lifestyle.

    Don't Buy Puppies Under 8 Weeks

    Young puppies need their mother. Always choose a puppy at least 8 weeks old.
